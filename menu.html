Minetta Tavern WV Burger

This Black Label Burger at Minetta Tavern is one of the most hyped burgers in the city. It consistently makes all the “best burger” lists, and it doesn’t disappoint. It’s made with a selection of prime dry-aged beef cuts, topped with caramelized onions, and served on a fluffy brioche bun that Balthazar Bakery made specifically for the masterpiece.

Price: $10.99

Red Hook Tavern Burger

From the team behind Hometown Bar-B-Que is Red Hook Tavern, a Brooklyn establishment who serves a juicy thick patty smothered in cheesy greatness. The Dry Aged Red Hook Tavern Burger (American cheese, white onion) will run you $29, but it’s worth every penny since the beef is such great quality. It comes with steak fries on the side too, which is a nice touch.

Price: $10.99



Jerrell’s BETR BRGR

Jerrell’s BETR BRGR got our Secret NYC stamp of approval, and it’s because of their flavorful plant-based burgers! Everything at this burger joint is vegan, including the O.G. BETR BRGR, which is our favorite! It’s made up of two Impossible™ patties, vegan cheese, lettuce & tomato, grilled onions, pickles & Jerrell’s special sauce. You can also try out the Supr BETR BRGR too, which adds their “bacon” and vegan chili on top…need we say more?!

Price: $10.99

JRaoul’s, SoHo

This burger is an if you know you know kind of deal! Raoul’s only serves 12 of their tasty burgers an evening so you got to try to catch them at a good and early time. The beefy patty comes on a challah roll with creamy cheese, watercress and cornichons, and you can only order it at the bar. It comes with a side of their authentic au poivre sauce, which we advise dipping your burger into as you devour. Take yourself on a solo dining date and enjoy all the flavors this burger has to offer…it’s worth it.


Price: $10.99
