Shangrila Resort

A mind-blowing and eye catching place having a theme of chinese kung fu masters home times , bamboo theme lodges with a lake in the middle not less them heaven having sky touching mountains on the background. After talking to the local staff we get to know that these are so old owned by a military pesonnel . It also has a state of the art resturant with every sort of crusines and dishes that excites your taste buds.

Shigar Fort

An ancient stone-made castle reminding you of ancient art and heritage . You can say that it is micro version of great wall of China.Now it is taken over my Pearl Continental hotel and you can enjoy tasty food with an ancient ambiance that has its own splendour.

Sarfranga Cold Desert

Sarfranga cold desert is a desert located on the southern side of Gilgit-Baltistan . A place to visit that is over the horizon where you can experience and see the earth to sky touching point. Its the same desrt that is cosen every year by Pakistan Car comumnity for Rally Racing named as Sarfranga Rally in which car enthusiasts from all around Pakistan buld and modifies their rides and participate in this.

Fairy Meadows
