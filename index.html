A Trip to Heavenly Beautiful Gilgit-Baltistan

Our Journey


A gossip was being carried out in our friend circle that Guys let's plan a tour somewhere this vactions some suggested a day trip plans to Murree , Galiyat but the query was it was our every week trip point and no one was eager to go there .Then suddenly one of our friends suggested that , Why shouldn't we go to Gilgit-Baltistan .The plan was not bad but initially everyone was assuming and was taking it as a folk that it couldn't happen but we were wrong.Afterwards when we thought and searched about the GB, its magnificient beauty, we were so eager to see the live heaven of Gilgit-Baltistan and we also really wanted to see what is actually there that people around the globe visit Pakistan just to visit Gilgit the plan becomes "Jana hai yahin Jana Hai warna Ni" and the journey started , We finalized the date of the very next month and started to calculate all the needfuls; expense, transport ,time of stay and etc etc. and finally we were completed with the planning stage and were waiting for the date , and at the end finally the day came
Morning was so pleasant , flowers blooming , birds chattering and we, loading aur needfuls to our ride and then set off to our jorney that started from Abbottabad . On the way , we went through beautiful mountain , cliffs ,vallies following natural fresh waterfalls that can eliminate your tiredness in an instance but despite of all we we think about our destination or excitement began to increase and we were in a haste that when will we reach there and finally after about 12 hours long drive we reached and our feet were finally on the land of GB and a gallery of new stories started.
